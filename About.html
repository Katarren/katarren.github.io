About me

This is a little bit about me and what I do. I know some people like to know about the artist, but if you are not one of those, feel free to skip this.

Basics

There is nothing in particular special about me; no tragic backstory like the artists of old.

I grew up and currently reside in Michigan, USA.

I am accepting of everyone and do not discriminate without reason. I am part of the LGBTQIA+ group myself, and go by any pronouns, though feminine pronouns are most common.

Education

After a typical public high school career, I went to Alma College to pursue a bachelors degree in art. I graduated in April of 2023.

During my time at Alma College, I learned the typical mediums of sketching, painting, sculpting, and more. I loved it, but nothing stuck out as much as the digital work I was already doing before that.

About my art career

I did little commission work throughout college, my main focus being on the pieces I had to make for classes. I did a commission for a proessor though, which will be included in my gallery, along with the pieces I had during my final presentation before graduating.

I did learn how to animate during my college career, something I am very proud of. So while I am new at animation, I am confident I will be improving as time goes on.
